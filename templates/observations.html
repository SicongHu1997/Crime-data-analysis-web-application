OBSERVATIONS AND HYPOTHESIS



Exponential Increase in Traffic Violations


Our foremost observation was the exponential increase in the amount of traffic
violations from 1998 onwards. This can be attributed to the delay in
the establishment of Road Laws and Rules in not only New South Wales
but across all of Australia.

From the pink colored line on the line chart at the right we can see
that there has been a sharp increase in 1998-1999 and a growth of almost 200%
has ensured every year. Despite this increase the amount of
deaths by road accidents has reduced, which is a positive indicator
regarding the implementation of these laws.





























Sudden Decrease in Robbery Offences

From the graph on the left one can observe the sharp decline in the amount
of Robbery offences (denoted by green line) . This can be characterised by
the introduction of stringent punishments for all types of crimes under this category.

More information regarding the crimes, offences and punishments for Robbery
can be found here.



















No Domino Effect from Crimes Anywhere else.

We also observed that significant global events which changed the course of humanity
did not have an impact on the crimes in Australia. Some recent events like 9/11 and the
2007-2008 GFC , which impacted a lot of countries around the world did not impact
crimes in Australia as much.

A possible explanation for such a behaviour is that Australia is still reliant upon
traditional industries like agriculture, mining and not so much on IT.
Also Australia has an extremely low population compared to other countries with similar
performance metrics and hence has a generally lower crime rate.
